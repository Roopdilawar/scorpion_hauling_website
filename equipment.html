Tandem

A tandem dump truck is a specialized type of dump truck that features two rear axles, providing increased load-carrying capacity and improved stability. Tandem dump trucks are commonly used in construction, mining, and other heavy-duty applications where larger payloads need to be transported. These trucks are designed to handle rough terrain and heavy loads, and they are capable of dumping their loads at the desired location using their tilting bed. Super B

A Super B train dump truck is a specialized configuration of dump truck commonly used in Canada, consisting of a tractor unit pulling two trailers connected in a B-train setup. The rear trailer is a dump trailer that can tilt and unload its contents at the desired location. With increased payload capacity and efficient transportation capabilities, Super B train dump trucks are popular for heavy-duty hauling of bulk materials like gravel, dirt, and sand. They require skilled drivers and compliance with local regulations, but are known for their productivity and versatility in various industries that require large-scale material transportation.

Quad

A quad dump truck is a specialized type of dump truck that features four rear axles, providing increased load-carrying capacity and improved stability. The quad axle configuration allows for higher weight limits and larger payloads, making it ideal for hauling heavy materials in construction, mining, and other demanding applications. Quad dump trucks are equipped with a hydraulic lift system that allows the bed to tilt and unload its contents at the desired location. With their robust design and high payload capacity, quad dump trucks are capable of efficiently transporting large volumes of materials, making them a popular choice for heavy-duty hauling tasks.

Tridem

A tridem dump truck is a specialized type of heavy-duty truck used for transporting loose materials, such as sand, gravel, or construction debris. It features a tri-axle configuration, with three axles in the rear, which provides increased carrying capacity and stability. The dump truck is equipped with a large open box or bed, known as the dump body, which can be raised hydraulically to unload its contents. Tridem dump trucks are commonly used in construction, mining, and other heavy-duty applications where large quantities of materials need to be transported efficiently and safely. They are typically operated by skilled drivers and require specialized training and licensing due to their size and weight.
